Low Latency


In the context of WebRTC Android, low latency is crucial for delivering high-quality real-time communication experiences. WebRTC Android employs several techniques to minimize latency and ensure smooth, real-time interactions.

  • Network Optimization: WebRTC Android optimizes network traffic by utilizing techniques such as congestion control and adaptive bitrate streaming. Congestion control algorithms monitor network conditions and adjust the bitrate of the transmitted audio and video streams to prevent overwhelming the available bandwidth. Adaptive bitrate streaming dynamically adjusts the quality of the transmitted media based on the available bandwidth, ensuring a smooth experience even in fluctuating network conditions.

By minimizing latency, WebRTC Android enhances the overall communication experience, making it suitable for a wide range of real-time applications, such as video conferencing, live streaming, and online gaming.

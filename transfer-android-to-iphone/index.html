Once you have everything you need, you can follow these steps to transfer your data from Android to iPhone:

  1. Connect your Android phone to your computer using the USB cable.
  2. Open iTunes on your computer.
  3. Click on the "Device" icon in the top left corner of the iTunes window.
  4. Click on the "Summary" tab.
  5. Click on the "Back Up Now" button.
  6. Once the backup is complete, click on the "Restore Backup" button.
  7. Select the backup you just created from the list of available backups.
  8. Click on the "Restore" button.
  9. The transfer process will begin. Depending on the amount of data you have, it may take some time to complete.
  10. Once the transfer is complete, your iPhone will restart.

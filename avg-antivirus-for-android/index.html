Tip 2: Schedule Regular Scans

In addition to real-time scanning, schedule regular in-depth scans of your device. This comprehensive scanning process thoroughly inspects your device for hidden threats that may have bypassed real-time detection.

AVG Antivirus for Android receives regular updates to its virus database. Keep your device protected by ensuring that you download and install these updates promptly.

Tip 4: Be Cautious When Downloading Apps

Exercise caution when downloading apps, especially from unknown sources. Only install apps from trusted app stores like the Google Play Store and pay attention to user reviews and ratings.

Public Wi-Fi networks can be vulnerable to security breaches and eavesdropping. Limit your use of public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like online banking or accessing personal accounts.

Create strong and unique passwords for your device and online accounts. Consider enabling two-factor authentication for added security, requiring both a password and a code sent to your phone for login.

Phishing scams attempt to trick you into revealing sensitive information like passwords or credit card numbers. Be wary of emails or messages from unknown senders and never click on suspicious links.

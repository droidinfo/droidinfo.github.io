Support for AirPlay

  • Convenience: AirPlay is a convenient way to share content from your Apple devices to your TV. You don't need any special cables or adapters, and you can start streaming with just a few taps.
  • Compatibility: AirPlay is compatible with a wide range of Apple devices, including the iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple TV. This means that you can share content from any of your Apple devices to your TV.
  • Quality: AirPlay streams content in high quality, so you can enjoy your favorite shows, movies, and games in stunning detail.
  • Security: AirPlay is a secure way to share content, as it uses encryption to protect your data.

Overall, support for AirPlay is a valuable feature of the Apple TV app. It allows users to easily and securely share content from their Apple devices to their TV.

To use screen mirroring on Android, you'll need a Miracast-compatible TV or monitor. Miracast is a wireless display standard that allows devices to connect and share screens without the need for cables or additional hardware.

Once you have a Miracast-compatible TV or monitor, you can follow these steps to mirror your Android device's screen:

  1. Open the Settings app on your Android device.
  2. Tap on the "Display" option.
  3. Tap on the "Cast" option.
  4. Select your Miracast-compatible TV or monitor from the list of available devices.

Your Android device's screen will now be mirrored on your TV or monitor. You can control your device from your TV or monitor's remote control, or you can use the touch controls on your device's screen.

Picture-in-Picture Mode


Picture-in-Picture mode is a new feature in Android 8 that allows you to watch videos in a small window while you're using other apps. This can be very useful for multitasking, such as watching a video while you're checking your email or browsing the web.

To use Picture-in-Picture mode, simply start playing a video in a supported app. Then, tap the Picture-in-Picture icon that appears in the top-right corner of the video player. The video will then shrink into a small window that you can move around the screen.

Picture-in-Picture mode is a great way to multitask on your Android device. It's perfect for watching videos while you're working, checking your email, or browsing the web.

Notification Channels


Notification channels are a new feature in Android 8 that give you more control over the notifications you receive. With notification channels, you can choose which apps can send you notifications, and you can customize the sound, vibration, and light settings for each channel.

This is a significant improvement over previous versions of Android, where you could only enable or disable notifications for an entire app. With notification channels, you can fine-tune your notification settings to ensure that you only receive the notifications that you want.

For example, you could create a notification channel for your email app and set it to only notify you of important emails. You could also create a notification channel for your social media apps and set it to only notify you of messages from your friends.

Notification channels are a powerful tool that can help you to manage your notifications and get the most out of your Android device.

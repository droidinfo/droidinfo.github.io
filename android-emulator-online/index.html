An Android emulator online is a software tool that simulates an Android device on your computer. It allows you to run and test Android apps on your computer without having to use a physical Android device.

There are several benefits to using an Android emulator online. For instance, it can help you test your apps on a variety of Android devices and configurations, identify and fix bugs more quickly, and develop apps more efficiently.

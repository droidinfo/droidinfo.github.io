IAPs are a common feature in free-to-play games, and they can be a significant source of revenue for developers.

  • Revenue model: IAPs can be a lucrative revenue model for developers, as they allow players to purchase virtual items that can enhance their gameplay experience. In the case of Angry Birds 2, IAPs have been a major contributor to the game's success, generating millions of dollars in revenue.
  • Player choice: IAPs give players the choice to spend money on the game in order to improve their gameplay experience. This can be a good option for players who want to progress more quickly or who want to access exclusive content. However, it's important to note that IAPs are not necessary to enjoy the game, and players can still have a great experience without spending any money.
  • Ethical concerns: There are some ethical concerns surrounding IAPs, particularly in games that are targeted at children. Overall, IAPs are a common feature in free-to-play games, and they can be a significant source of revenue for developers.

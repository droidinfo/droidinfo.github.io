What Phones Are Android Phones?

Android phones are a type of smartphone that uses the Android operating system. Android is an open-source operating system, which means that it is free for manufacturers to use. This has led to a wide variety of Android phones being available on the market, from budget-friendly options to high-end flagships.

  • Key aspect: Wide range of manufacturers

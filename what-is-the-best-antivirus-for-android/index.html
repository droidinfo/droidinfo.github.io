In the realm of cybersecurity, real-time protection stands as a cornerstone of defense against emerging threats. For Android devices, which are constantly exposed to a barrage of malware and other malicious software, real-time protection is an indispensable feature of any comprehensive antivirus solution.

This constant vigilance ensures that threats are detected and neutralized before they can compromise the device or its data.

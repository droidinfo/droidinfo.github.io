Resolution


The resolution of a video refers to the number of pixels that make up the image. The higher the resolution, the sharper and more detailed the image will be. 4K HDR is a video resolution that is four times the resolution of 1080p HD. HDR stands for high dynamic range, which means that the video has a wider range of colors and contrasts than standard video. This results in a more realistic and lifelike image.

YouTube Android TV supports video resolutions up to 4K HDR. This means that you can watch your favorite YouTube videos in the highest possible quality. 4K HDR video is especially beneficial for watching movies and TV shows, as it provides a more immersive and cinematic experience.

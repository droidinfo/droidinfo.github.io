Is Samsung an Android Phone?

Yes, Samsung phones are Android phones. Samsung uses the Android operating system on all of its Galaxy smartphones. This means that Samsung phones have access to all of the same apps and features as other Android phones. However, Samsung does make some modifications to the Android operating system on its phones. These modifications are called the Samsung Experience. The Samsung Experience includes a number of features that are not available on other Android phones, such as the Edge Panel and the S Pen.

Improved accessibility


The integration of improved accessibility features in Samsung Android P is a significant step towards promoting inclusivity and empowering individuals with disabilities to fully utilize mobile technology. These features address various accessibility needs, enhancing the overall user experience for all.

  • Enhanced Screen Reader: TalkBack, a screen reader, provides spoken feedback for users with visual impairments. It reads aloud text, describes images, and announces actions, enabling users to navigate and interact with their devices independently.
  • Magnification: Magnification allows users to enlarge the screen, making it easier to read text and see details. This feature is particularly beneficial for users with low vision or visual impairments.
  • High Contrast Mode: High Contrast Mode adjusts the color scheme to enhance the visibility of text and icons. It reduces eye strain and improves readability for users with color blindness or low vision.
  • Live Caption: Live Caption automatically generates captions for audio and video content, providing real-time accessibility for users with hearing impairments or in noisy environments.

These accessibility features in Samsung Android P not only meet the diverse needs of users with disabilities but also align with the growing demand for inclusive technology. By providing accessible and user-friendly options, Samsung demonstrates its commitment to creating a more equitable and empowering mobile experience for all.

Improved battery life


The enhanced battery life in Samsung Android P is a significant improvement that directly impacts the user experience. With the introduction of new features like Adaptive Battery and Power Saving Mode, Samsung addresses the growing demand for extended battery life in mobile devices.

Adaptive Battery utilizes machine learning to analyze usage patterns and optimize battery consumption. It identifies apps that are rarely used and restricts their background activity, reserving power for the apps that are frequently used. This intelligent power management extends the overall battery life of the device.

Power Saving Mode takes a more aggressive approach to conserving battery life when the device's power is running low. It limits background activities, reduces screen brightness, and restricts data usage. This mode is ideal for situations when the device needs to last for an extended period without the possibility of immediate charging.

The combination of Adaptive Battery and Power Saving Mode provides users with a comprehensive solution for managing their battery life. Whether they prioritize long-term optimization or immediate power conservation, Samsung Android P offers the necessary tools to meet their needs.

This improved battery life is particularly beneficial for users who rely heavily on their smartphones throughout the day. With Samsung Android P, they can enjoy extended usage without worrying about running out of power, enhancing their overall mobile experience.

New Bixby features


The integration of new Bixby features in Samsung Android P represents a significant step forward in the evolution of Samsung's virtual assistant. Bixby Routines and Bixby Vision enhance the user experience by automating tasks and providing visual information, respectively.

Bixby Routines allows users to create automated actions based on specific triggers. For example, a user can create a routine that turns on Wi-Fi and opens the music app when they connect their headphones. This feature streamlines everyday tasks and saves time.

Bixby Vision utilizes the device's camera to provide visual information. By pointing the camera at an object, users can access information about it, such as product reviews, landmarks, or translations. This feature enhances the user's ability to interact with the world around them.

The combination of Bixby Routines and Bixby Vision makes Samsung Android P a more intelligent and user-friendly operating system. These features empower users to automate tasks, access visual information, and enhance their overall mobile experience.

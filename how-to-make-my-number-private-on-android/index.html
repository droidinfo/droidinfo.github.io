Caller ID


Caller ID is a feature that allows the recipient of a phone call to see the phone number of the caller. This can be a useful feature for businesses and individuals who want to be easily identifiable, but it can also be a privacy concern for those who want to keep their phone number private.

Caller ID blocking is a feature that allows you to prevent your phone number from being displayed when you call someone. This can be a useful feature for a variety of reasons, such as:

  • Protecting your privacy
  • Avoiding unwanted calls
  • Making prank calls

To enable caller ID blocking on Android, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Phone app.
  2. Tap the three dots in the top-right corner.
  3. Tap Settings.
  4. Tap Calls.
  5. Tap Additional settings.
  6. Tap Caller ID.
  7. Select Hide number.

Once you have enabled caller ID blocking, your phone number will not be displayed when you call someone. However, it is important to note that caller ID blocking is not foolproof. Some phone carriers and services may still be able to display your phone number, even if you have enabled caller ID blocking.

If you are concerned about your privacy, you may want to consider using a different method to make your number private, such as using a VoIP service or a disposable phone number.

Privacy Settings


Your Android phone's privacy settings give you control over who can see your phone number. This is important because it allows you to protect your privacy and prevent unwanted calls and messages.

To adjust your privacy settings, open the Settings app on your phone and tap on "Privacy." Then, tap on "Phone" and then on "Caller ID." You will see two options:

  • Show my caller ID: This option allows anyone to see your phone number when you call them.
  • Hide my caller ID: This option prevents anyone from seeing your phone number when you call them.

If you are concerned about your privacy, we recommend that you select the "Hide my caller ID" option. This will prevent your phone number from being displayed when you call someone.

In addition to hiding your caller ID, you can also block specific phone numbers from calling or messaging you. To do this, open the Phone app on your phone and tap on the "Recent" tab. Then, tap on the phone number that you want to block and then tap on the "Block" button.

By adjusting your privacy settings and blocking unwanted phone numbers, you can take control of your privacy and protect your phone number from unwanted calls and messages.
